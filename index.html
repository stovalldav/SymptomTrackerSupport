About Symptom Tracker

Symptom Tracker is a secure, privacy-focused iOS application designed specifically for tracking daily health symptoms for medical reporting, particularly for Veterans Affairs (VA) documentation. The app provides comprehensive logging, trend analysis, and professional PDF exports while maintaining complete user privacy.

100% Private: All your health data stays on your device. No data collection, no cloud storage, no third-party access.

Key Features

Severity Tracking

Rate all symptoms on a comprehensive 0-10 severity scale with intuitive sliders for precise tracking.

Draft Saving

Save your progress throughout the day and edit previous entries. Never lose your symptom data.

Professional PDF Export

Export individual logs, summary reports, and custom date ranges in professional PDF format.

Trend Analysis

View interactive line charts showing symptom patterns across daily, weekly, and monthly timeframes.

Biometric Security

Protect your health data with PIN authentication and biometric access (Face ID/Touch ID).

Native iOS App

Built specifically for iOS with native performance, accessibility, and seamless user experience.

How to Use

Set Up Security

On first launch, create a 4-digit PIN to secure your health data. Enable biometric authentication for quick access.

Log Daily Symptoms

Navigate to "Daily Log" and use the severity sliders to rate each symptom from 0-10. Add detailed notes for context.

Save and Edit

Save drafts throughout the day to build complete entries. Edit previous entries by tapping them in the "Entry List".

Analyze Trends

View the "Summary" tab to see statistics and interactive charts showing your symptom patterns over time.

Export for Medical Use

Use the "Export" tab to generate professional PDF reports for your healthcare provider or VA appointments.

Privacy & Security

Your Health Data Privacy is Our Top Priority

  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or cloud services
  • No analytics, tracking, or data collection of any kind
  • App is protected with PIN and biometric authentication
  • Data is encrypted using iOS built-in security features
  • You have complete control over your health information
  • No third-party data sharing or advertising
  • No personal health information ever leaves your device
  • No cloud storage or automatic backup (manual export only)
Data Ownership: You own and control 100% of your health data. We never see, access, or store any of your personal health information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data secure?

Yes, absolutely. All your health data is stored locally on your device using iOS security features. The app is protected with PIN and biometric authentication. No data ever leaves your device unless you manually export it.

Can I use this for VA disability claims?

Yes, the app is specifically designed for VA medical reporting. The PDF exports are professionally formatted and include all the detailed symptom tracking information typically required for VA documentation.

What happens if I lose my device?

Since data is stored locally, losing your device means losing your data unless you've manually exported backups. We recommend regularly exporting your data as PDF for backup purposes.

Can I share data with my doctor?

Yes! The app's PDF export feature creates professional reports that you can easily share with healthcare providers. You have complete control over what data you share and when.

Is there a subscription or cost?

Symptom Tracker is designed to be accessible for all users. Check the App Store for current pricing information.
