About Symptom Tracker for Veterans

Symptom Tracker for Veterans is a secure, privacy-focused iOS application designed specifically for veterans to track daily health symptoms for VA medical reporting and disability claims documentation. The app provides comprehensive logging, trend analysis, and professional PDF exports while maintaining complete user privacy and meeting the specific needs of veteran healthcare.

100% Private: All your health data stays on your device. No data collection, no cloud storage, no third-party access.

VA PTSD Assessment

Symptom Tracker for Veterans includes a comprehensive PTSD questionnaire specifically designed around VA disability rating criteria to help veterans document their symptoms accurately for medical evaluations and C&P exams.

Core Symptoms Tracked:

Psychological Symptoms

  • Anxiety and Depression levels (0-10 scale)
  • Panic attack frequency (None to Daily)
  • Sleep impairment severity
  • Memory issues assessment

PTSD-Specific Symptoms

  • Hypervigilance intensity
  • Emotional numbing severity
  • Flashback frequency and intensity
  • Nightmare occurrences
  • Avoidance behavior patterns

Functional Impact

  • Occupational impairment (None to Total)
  • Social impairment assessment
  • Medication requirements
  • Daily functioning capabilities

Documentation Fields:

The app provides detailed note fields for comprehensive documentation:

  • Symptom Triggers: Document environmental, situational, or emotional triggers
  • Coping Strategies: Record what helps manage symptoms effectively

  • Medication Notes: Monitor medication effectiveness, side effects, and changes

VA-Aligned Documentation: This structured assessment helps veterans document PTSD symptoms in alignment with VA evaluation criteria for more accurate disability ratings and comprehensive medical records.

How to Use

Set Up Security

On first launch, create a 4-digit PIN to secure your health data. Enable biometric authentication for quick access.

Log Daily Symptoms

Navigate to "Daily Log" and use the severity sliders to rate each symptom from 0-10. Add detailed notes for context.

Save and Edit

Save drafts throughout the day to build complete entries. Edit previous entries by tapping them in the "Entry List".

Analyze Trends

View the "Summary" tab to see statistics and interactive charts showing your symptom patterns over time.

Export for Medical Use

Use the "Export" tab to generate professional PDF reports for your healthcare provider or VA appointments.

Data Export Safety & Best Practices

Symptom Tracker for Veterans offers both PDF and CSV export options. Understanding the security implications of each format helps protect your sensitive health data.

Export Options:

PDF Exports (Recommended for Sharing)

  • Professional formatting for healthcare providers
  • Cannot be easily modified or corrupted
  • Suitable for VA appointments and medical records
  • More secure for sharing sensitive information

CSV Exports (Use with Caution)

  • Raw data in plain text format
  • Best for personal backup and data migration
  • Allows data import back into the app
  • Contains unencrypted health information
Important: CSV Data Safety

CSV files contain your complete health data in unencrypted plain text. To protect your privacy:

CSV Safety Guidelines:

  • Only share CSV files with trusted healthcare providers
  • Delete CSV files from shared devices after use
  • Use secure methods when transferring files (encrypted email, secure cloud storage)
  • Never save CSV files to public or unsecured locations
  • Consider password-protecting folders containing CSV exports
  • Use PDF exports instead of CSV when sharing with healthcare providers
Best Practice: Use CSV exports primarily for personal backup purposes. For maximum security when sharing data with healthcare providers or the VA, use PDF exports instead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data secure?

Yes, absolutely. All your health data is stored locally on your device using iOS security features. The app is protected with PIN and biometric authentication. No data ever leaves your device unless you manually export it.

Can I use this for VA disability claims?

Absolutely! Symptom Tracker for Veterans is specifically designed for VA disability claims and medical reporting. The PDF exports are professionally formatted and include all the detailed symptom tracking information required for VA documentation, disability claims, and C&P exams.

What happens if I lose my device?

Since data is stored locally, losing your device means losing your data unless you've manually exported backups. We recommend regularly exporting your data as PDF for backup purposes.

Can I share data with my doctor?

Yes! The app's PDF export feature creates professional reports that you can easily share with healthcare providers. You have complete control over what data you share and when.

How does the PTSD questionnaire help with VA claims?

The PTSD questionnaire is specifically designed around VA disability rating criteria from 38 CFR 4.130. It tracks core symptoms like anxiety, depression, panic attacks, sleep issues, hypervigilance, flashbacks, and functional impairments. The detailed documentation fields help veterans provide comprehensive evidence for C&P exams and disability claims by tracking symptoms, triggers, coping strategies, therapy progress, and medication effectiveness over time.

Can I import data from CSV files?

Yes! The app supports importing CSV files that were previously exported from Symptom Tracker for Veterans. This allows you to restore data from backups or transfer data between devices. The app validates imported data and creates automatic backups before import to prevent data loss. You can import CSV files using the "Import from CSV" button in the Export tab.

What's the difference between PDF and CSV exports?

PDF exports are professionally formatted, secure, and ideal for sharing with healthcare providers or VA personnel. CSV exports contain raw data in plain text format and are best for personal backup or data analysis. Important: CSV files contain unencrypted health information, so use PDF exports when sharing sensitive data and follow security best practices when handling CSV files.

Is there a subscription or cost?

Symptom Tracker for Veterans is designed to be accessible for all veterans. Check the App Store for current pricing information. We believe essential health tracking tools should be available to those who served our country.
